HerVoice Rwanda

Empowering Women & Girls to Speak Out Against Violence

A Safe Space to Be Heard

HerVoice is a digital platform designed to support women and girls who experience violence or harassment. Many victims remain silent due to fear, stigma, or lack of access to reporting channels. This silence allows violence to continue, causing harm to individuals and society.

We provide a safe, confidential, and accessible way for victims to report cases of violence without the need to appear in person.

Key Features

Anonymous Reporting

Submit complaints without revealing your identity if you choose. Your privacy is our priority.

Organization Selection

Choose the NGO or organization you trust to report to. Work with local and international partners.

Evidence Upload

Attach photos, videos, or audio recordings as proof to strengthen your case.

Secure Communication

All information is stored securely using encryption to protect your privacy.

Easy Access

Access the platform through mobile phone or computer - anytime, anywhere.

Follow-up System

Track your case and receive updates as partner organizations respond efficiently.
