HerVoice Rwanda

Empowering Women & Girls to Speak Out Against Violence

A Safe Space to Be Heard

HerVoice is a digital platform designed to support women and girls who experience violence or harassment. Many victims remain silent due to fear, stigma, or lack of access to reporting channels. This silence allows violence to continue, causing harm to individuals and society.

We provide a safe, confidential, and accessible way for victims to report cases of violence without the need to appear in person.
